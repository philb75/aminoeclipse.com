How can Amino Eclipse keep horses racing and delay exhaustion?

For a horse, getting exhausted is due to the brain serotonin “pulling the emergency break”. The flush of serotonin depends on the balance between Tryptophan and Branch Chain Amino Acids (BCAA)* circulating in the brain.

Amino Eclipse provides the correct balance of BCAA and Tryptophan to increase BCCA circulating in the brain and postpones the production of serotonin. As a result, it delays the “emergency break” and the feeling of exhaustion and keeps your horse’s will power to go on racing.

Daily dose of 20 to 40 g of Amino Eclipse (depending on the extent of effort required and horse condition):
– brings additional sprint power
– delays the occurrence of exhaustion and lets the horse recover faster from fatigue
